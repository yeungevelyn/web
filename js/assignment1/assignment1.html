  1. Log into SOLS
  2. Select 'Current Session' from the left-hand navigation menu.
  3. Select 'Academic Consideration' from the drop-down list.
  4. Select 'Create a new application' and read the information and instructions.
  5. When prompted, provide information about:
    1. The subject and assessment tasks you are requesting consideration for (if you aren't sure of the information, you can check your subject outline).
    2. The nature of assistance (type of consideration) you are seeking (e.g. extension of time to submit a task).
    3. Your circumstances.
  6. When prompted, choose the category of supporting documentation that applies to you:
    1. Provide documentation now.
    2. Provide documentation within three (3) working days of the task due date - you will need to return to your application and add document/s no later than three (3) working days after the due date. The documentation must cover the period you are requesting consideration for.
    3. Supporting documentation not required – only choose this if you are eligible to self-certify your application.
  7. If you are required to provide supporting documentation, when prompted:
    1. Select the document type from the dropdown.
    2. Attach the document.
    3. Complete all mandatory fields.
    4. Read and agree to the terms.
    5. Click upload.
  8. If required, add the next subject and assessment task you are requesting consideration for, and follow steps 5-7 above.
  9. After you have selected all the subjects and assessment tasks you are requesting consideration for, read and accept the declaration.
  10. Submit your application OR save it as a draft to return to it later.
